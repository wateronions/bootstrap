It's a dog!

Dogs are amazing companions, and here are a few funny facts about them:

  • Some dogs can learn more than 100 words—basically, they're furry little geniuses!
  • In ancient Egypt, some dogs were buried with their owners, almost like ancient doggy aristocrats!
